Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1/04/2024

In brief: We are TradeLab (“TradeLab,” “we” or “us”). We respect your privacy and work hard to protect the confidentiality of information that you give us. We will NOT share your personal data with third parties unless that it is explicitly requested by you and it's needed for the provision of services and data you have requested (explained in detail in this privacy policy).

Updates

We may update this privacy policy at our discretion from time to time by posting a new version on our website. You should check our website occasionally to ensure any changes are suitable for you.

Personal data that we collect

  1. User Accounts: If you decide to create an account with TraderSync, you will need to provide us some personal data so we can provide you with the services. Currently, during the registration you will be required to provide:
    1. Email
    2. Username
    3. First name
  2. Orders: When you make a purchase or attempt to make a purchase through the Site, we collect certain information from you, including your full name, billing address, payment information (including credit card numbers, email address, and phone number).This information is collected by our payment processor and solely kept for billing purposes.
  3. Contact Data: We might process certain data you provide us when communicating with us via email such as Operating System, Browser, and Device type for the purpose of fixing potential issues with the platform.
  4. Cookies and other technologies: Correspond to data files that are placed on your browser and often include anonymous unique identifiers.

How do we use your personal information?

We will process your personal data as necessary for certain legitimate business interest, which include the following:

Profile

User account and profile operations
We use your contact details, such as username, email address, first name, in the following ways:

  • to authenticate your user account, ensure its security along with the security of our services, and to prevent spam, fraud, and abuse;
  • to acknowledge, confirm, and deal with your user account;
  • to send all new users a welcome activation email to verify their email address;

User account deleting

You may refuse to use our Website and/or application and may ask us to delete your account in the Profile settings. Your account will be deleted immediately together with any subsequent personal data you have supplied to us or we have collected. This action is irreversible.

Customer Service

When you contact us for support,we may process the data that you might have provided us to properly solve any issues you might encounter.

API

If you are using our API (Application Programming Interface) services (such importing trades, exporting reports), then we may process your personal data in order to provide and support those services.

When would we share personal information

We share your personal data with third parties in the following situations:

    • Business transfer: If we sell our business or our company assets are acquired by a third party, personal data held by us about our general account users, subscription account users or customer may be one of the transferred assets.
    • Administrative and legal reasons: If we need to disclose your personal data to comply with a legal obligation and/or judicial or regulatory proceedings, a court order or other legal process, to enforce our Terms of Use, House Rules or other applicable contract terms that you are subject to, or to protect us or our contractors against loss or damage. This may include, but is not limited to, exchanging information with the police, courts or law enforcement organizations.

Public information on our site

Public Profile

Upon creating an account with us, all your information by default is set to private. Meaning no one else have access to view your information. You can decide to share trade(s) with the public if you wish. By doing so, you are responsible for what you share.

Public Content

There is no public content on our site. Every profile is private by default and no one other than you, the user, can view your profile.

Security of personal data

We use technical procedures to protect your personal data.

We use Transport Layer Security (TLS) encryption technology in order to protect certain information that you submit to us. This technology protects you from having your information intercepted by anyone while it is being transmitted to TradeLab. While on a secure page, the “lock” icon in the browser window is displayed, confirming that a secure and encrypted connection has been established with the Website. We work hard to ensure that our Service is secure and that we meet industry standards. We also use other safeguards, such as firewalls, authentication systems (i.e. passwords, and personal identification numbers), and access control mechanisms to control unauthorized access to systems and data. If you have chosen to create an account, you are responsible for doing everything you reasonably can to keep your access details secret. You must not share these details with anyone else.

We also do our best to protect user information offline. All of our users’ personal information is restricted to our offices. This information is only accessed in order to provide our services or to provide technical support.

The servers that store personal data are located in a secure environment in a locked facility.

We are constantly improving the ways we secure, store, and process the data we collect, including the addition of physical security measures that help us counter unauthorized access to our systems. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children and sensitive personal data

This website and mobile apps are not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly contact or collect personal information from children below age of 18.

Third-Party Services and Integrations

TradingView Integration

Our platform integrates TradingView widgets to provide chart visualization and market data features. We want to be clear about our relationship with TradingView:

  • TradeLab and TradingView are separate entities. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected with TradingView in any way.
  • We do not share any personal information, user data, or trading information with TradingView.
  • The TradingView widgets are embedded in our platform solely as a feature for our users' convenience.
  • When you interact with TradingView widgets, you may be subject to TradingView's own privacy policy and terms of service.
  • We recommend reviewing TradingView's privacy policy to understand how they may collect and use data through their widgets.
